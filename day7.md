Here's the quick summary of what the strategy recommends:
Your Primary Model → Claude Sonnet 4.6
Perfect for a daily student who codes and researches. Smart, fast, and reliable for 80% of your tasks.
Your Default Effort → Standard
Thorough enough for most work. Only bump it up when the stakes are higher.
The Simple Rule to Remember:
Haiku → Quick lookups, flashcards, one-liners
Sonnet → Daily coding, studying, research (your main model)
Opus → Final projects, hard algorithms, high-stakes submissions
