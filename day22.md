# AI Career Twin - Startup Validation Input

## Startup Idea

AI Career Twin is an AI-powered career intelligence platform that acts as a user's professional digital twin. It analyzes resumes, LinkedIn profiles, portfolios, GitHub repositories, and industry trends to provide personalized career guidance, skill-gap analysis, job recommendations, and growth roadmaps.

## Problem Being Solved

Many students and professionals struggle with career planning because they don't know:

* Which skills are currently in demand
* What to learn next
* Which jobs they qualify for
* How to increase their salary potential
* How to stay competitive in an AI-driven job market

Career advice is often generic, outdated, or expensive. AI Career Twin provides personalized, data-driven career guidance.

## Why I Want to Build It

As technology evolves rapidly, many people feel uncertain about their career direction. I want to create a platform that helps individuals make informed career decisions using AI-powered insights rather than guesswork.

The goal is to help people identify opportunities, close skill gaps, and achieve professional growth more efficiently.
