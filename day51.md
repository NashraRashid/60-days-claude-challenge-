# Day 51 – Defend Your Experience 🛡️

## Overview

For Day 51 of the **60 Days Claude Challenge**, I designed the Product Requirements Document (PRD) for **Defend Your Experience**, an AI-powered Interview Defense Simulator.

The project focuses on helping final-year Computer Science students and recent graduates prepare for interviews by transforming their resumes into personalized interview sessions.

---

## Problem Statement

Many students use AI tools to create polished resumes but struggle to confidently explain or defend the claims they make during interviews.

The goal of this project is to bridge the gap between resume creation and interview readiness.

---

## Features

- Resume Upload (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
- Resume Text Input
- Claim Extraction
- Adaptive Interview Engine
- Smart Follow-up Questions
- Confidence Scoring
- Defense Report
- Personalized Feedback
- Practice Recommendations
- Privacy-first Browser Processing

---

## Target Users

- Final-year Computer Science students
- Recent Software Engineering graduates
- Internship applicants
- Entry-level job seekers

---

## User Flow

1. Upload or paste resume
2. Extract important claims
3. Generate adaptive interview questions
4. Conduct chat-style interview
5. Analyze responses
6. Generate Defense Report
7. Suggest improvements

---

## Skills Learned

- Product Requirements Documentation (PRD)
- Product Thinking
- AI Workflow Design
- Prompt Engineering
- User Experience Design
- Frontend System Planning

---

## Technologies

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
- Claude AI
- Prompt Engineering

---

## Key Takeaway

A great resume gets an interview, but the ability to confidently defend your experience is what ultimately builds trust with recruiters.

Designing this project helped me think beyond coding and focus on building AI products that solve real-world problems.
