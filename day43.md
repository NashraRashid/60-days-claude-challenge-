# Day 43 – AI Workflow Architect (Small Business Website)

## Project

AI Workflow Architect – Small Business Website

## Objective

Build an interactive workflow management application that guides developers through the complete lifecycle of creating a small business website, from client discovery to deployment and project handoff.

---

## Features

- Complete Website Development Workflow
- Client Discovery & Requirements Gathering
- Sitemap & Content Strategy
- Design System & UI Planning
- HTML, CSS & JavaScript Development Guide
- Accessibility & Performance Checklist
- Deployment & Client Handoff Guide
- AI Prompt Library for Every Stage
- Interactive Progress Tracking
- Bookmark & Notes System

---

## Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
- Claude AI

---

## What I Learned

- Planning is a critical part of successful web development.
- AI can assist throughout the entire software development lifecycle—not just with coding.
- Organizing development into structured stages improves productivity and project quality.
- Accessibility, SEO, testing, and deployment should be considered from the beginning of a project.
- Interactive dashboards and checklists make project management more efficient.

---

## Challenges Faced

- Designing a complete end-to-end workflow.
- Organizing multiple development stages into a user-friendly interface.
- Creating reusable prompts and productivity tools.
- Managing progress tracking and interactive navigation.

---

## Outcome

Successfully developed an AI-powered Workflow Architect that helps developers and freelancers manage website projects efficiently through structured workflows, AI-generated prompts, interactive checklists, and deployment guidance.

---



## Key Takeaway

Great websites are built through a structured process, not just great code. This project showed how AI can support every stage of development—from planning and design to testing, deployment, and client handoff—making workflows more organized, efficient, and professional.
